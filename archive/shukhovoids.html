The Generic Sublime undertakes the project of radical integration of the skyscraper and takes it to the level of the ultimate post-urban megalomania: a complex building that organizes the territory in a synthetic field of competition and synergy. The Generic Sublime upgrades the organizational capabilities of the skyscraper by exploring the selfsurmounting form that the anti-urban takes when assembled in a collective.

work
Spring 2013: The Generic Sublime, Utopioids, Professor Ciro Najle

This project begins with a study of Russian Constructivism as a utopoian project with Vladimir Shukhov’s Radio Tower as a model of this utopia to be exploited and remodeled to its “post-urban” limits inherent in both it as a structure and general Lenin- era project in the creation of a “utopioid” prototype.

Information of surrounding wireless networks was first collected and mapped to their spaces. KisMAC was used in the collection of this data, providing network SSIDs, strength, signal to noise ratio and encryption information. This software is made for hacking into vulnerable networks, so its use in this project subverts its purpose, but more importantly it provides the proof for the necessity of the awareness the project provides. Once the data is collected, it is run through Processing to be pruned and organized into relevant information. The resulting spreadsheets of data are then read by a script built in Max/MSP which generates the resulting audio signals/images of the spaces.
